Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2025

1. Introduction

At CalBurn, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our calorie burning calculator service.

2. Information We Don't Collect

CalBurn is designed with privacy in mind. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Store your calculations or results
  • Track your activity history outside your browser
  • Use cookies for tracking purposes

3. Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to:

  • Save your activity history for your convenience
  • Store your preferences
  • Improve your user experience

This data never leaves your device and is completely under your control.

4. Calculations

All calculations are performed locally in your browser. Your weight, height, and activity information are never transmitted to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Font Awesome for icons
  • Google Fonts for typography
  • Chart.js for data visualization

These services may collect basic usage data. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

6. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our service.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
