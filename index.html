About Calories Burned Calculator

Our calories burned calculator helps you estimate the number of calories you burn during various physical activities and exercises. Whether you're walking, running, cycling, swimming or doing other activities, this calculator provides accurate estimates based on your weight, activity type, intensity level and duration.

1

Enter Weight

2

Select Activity

Choose your exercise type from our comprehensive list of activities

3

4

Add Duration

5

Get Results
